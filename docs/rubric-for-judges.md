# Judges Rubric (Security/Engineering Maturity)

Teams can earn bonus points by showing:
- A **DFD** with trust boundaries
- At least **1 STRIDE threat per category**
- A short, prioritized mitigation plan

Suggested scoring:
- 1 point: DFD exists and is understandable
- 1 point: Trust boundaries are marked
- 2 points: STRIDE threats documented (>= 6 total)
- 2 points: Mitigations mapped to threats (not generic)
- 1 point: Evidence of implementation (e.g., RBAC middleware, logging, rate limiting)
- 1 point: Clear explanation of tradeoffs
