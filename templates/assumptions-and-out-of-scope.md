# Assumptions & Out of Scope

Use this to keep your threat model realistic for a hackathon.

## Common assumptions
- We use HTTPS for all traffic
- We store secrets in environment variables (not in the repo)
- We use a managed database (or local DB for demo)

## Out of scope examples
- Full penetration testing
- Advanced WAF/CDN configuration
- Enterprise identity governance
